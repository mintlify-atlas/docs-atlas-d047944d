LocalAudio Subtitles with Subtitle Engine is a modification to TheNexusAvenger's LocalAudioSubtitles that replaces the native subtitle renderer with Norteous's Subtitle Engine. You get all the powerful subtitle configuration of LocalAudio — speakers, macros, levels, and rolloff detection — while your players see polished, animated subtitles rendered by Subtitle Engine.

## How it works

This module sits between LocalAudio's event system and your players' screens. When LocalAudio fires a `ShowSubtitle` event for an audio track, this module intercepts it and calls Subtitle Engine's `localSubtitle` function instead of rendering a native Roblox GUI.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Replace the module">
    Swap out your existing `LocalAudioSubtitles` module with this one inside your `Packages` folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ensure Subtitle Engine is installed">
    Place Subtitle Engine in `ReplicatedStorage`. The "Self" profile must exist with no speaker name configured.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your subtitle data">
    Create a `SubtitleDataModule` defining speakers, macros, and levels. Pass it to `LocalAudioSubtitles:SetUp()`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy animated subtitles">
    Players see Subtitle Engine's polished display — with typing animations, speaker colors, and proper timing — synced to your audio.
  </Step>
</Steps>
